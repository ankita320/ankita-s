a little about me

About me.

I'm a Bengali-American senior born and raised in Queens. I fell in love with coding during quarantine — and that curiosity never left. I write for the Stuyvesant Spectator, do color-pencil realism, and spend too much time in cafés.

I volunteer at my local library every week. I'm a Mitchell who gives off Alex energy. I believe the best work sits at the intersection of code and craft.
